"AI Kombat Arcade Machine" by Alena Zolotareva, generated with Rodin (Hyper3D).
Custom model made for this project.

Modifications: exported to Draco-compressed .glb; cabinet body (STEEL3) and
neon trim (NEON) recolored to the site palette; the SCREEN material is replaced
at runtime with a live CRT attract shader; the ARCADE marquee is replaced with a
runtime "AI KOMBAT" glow texture; the GLASS panel is dimmed.
